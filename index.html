In the sprawl of the 'verse, HELIOS operates as a collective of pilots and specialists who value reliability, teamwork, and a good time in equal measure. We're not a militia, not a megacorp — just a crew that takes pride in getting the job done and enjoying the journey.

We maintain a dual focus on combat and industrial gameplay, blending both into unified operations:

Combat — security escorts, fleet engagements, bounty work, rapid-response support
Industrial — mining, logistics, cargo, refuel/resupply, and salvage
Combined Arms — combat wings clear the field while industrial crews recover, refine, and profit

Our goal isn't to be the biggest org — it's to create memorable, social, and genuinely fun experiences. We build enough structure to keep things organized, but not so much that you're filling out forms to undock.

We do not run official piracy operations, and anyone using the HELIOS name to ruin someone else's night won't be wearing the tag for long. Beyond that — come as you are, fly what you fly, and help keep things drama-free.
